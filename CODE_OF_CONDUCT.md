# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- The use of sexualised language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Responsible Security Research

Given the nature of this project, we additionally expect:

- All security tools and techniques discussed are used only in authorised, controlled environments
- No sharing of exploits, credentials, or access to systems without authorisation
- Vulnerability disclosures follow the process outlined in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
